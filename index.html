2
⚠ REQUIRED — Select ORCA OFFICIAL LOGO
Mandatory action #1 · Cannot skip
▼

On the product page for your item, you'll see a logo dropdown menu. It lists logo options.

  1. Choose your size and color.
  2. Open the logo dropdown.
  3. Select ORCA OFFICIAL LOGO — this is the only approved option.
  4. Add the item to your cart.
What this does: Selecting the logo automatically adds the logo as a separate line item in your cart. You do not need to add the logo separately. If you don't see it on the item after checkout, something went wrong — call the vendor.

⚠ Do not order "without logo" — that requires calling the vendor at 541-773-7121 and is not how CIMT orders are set up.

3
⚠ REQUIRED — Add the $25 S&H item to your cart
Mandatory action #2 · This is why orders got cancelled
▼

Shipping is a separate product, not a checkout calculation. The vendor named the category "Richardson Hat S&H" — ignore the hat reference, it applies to all shipped orders.

How to add it:

  1. Click the button below. It takes you straight to the S&H product page on the vendor site.
  2. On that page, click the vendor's "Add to Shopping Cart" button.
  3. Add quantity 1 per order — not per shirt.
→ Open S&H product page
Confirmed price: $25.00 — stated on the S&H product page as a "one time fee" covering shipping the order to you. (The homepage banner says $21.50; that figure appears to be outdated. The product page is authoritative. Confirm with the vendor if in doubt.)

Picking up locally instead of shipping? Skip this step but call the vendor first to confirm pickup is available.
